## Gods and Elements

Gods are beings who are born or embody elemental powers, however, not all elements have a deity, and not all elements are equal, some Gods have devout followings that are favoured from time to time depending on the deity's mood, while others are more obscure and simply thank their existence.
### **Holy Goddess Aryllis - Embodiment of Light**

This is the goddess almost every human and monster knows, and those who are unaware of her existence are said to be struck down or shunned.

As an embodiment of the Holy light element, she stands in direct opposition to the goddess of the Dark element, Viyella, standing with her as the two main Goddesses of the world.

Aryllis is known for her Xenophobia for any living being that is a descendant of the Dark goddess, meaning any non-animal or non-human being. She presents herself as "The Goddess of creation" and the most powerful of them all, despite being equally matched by Viyella.

She Is said to come down to churches when boys come of age to give them a blessing that would make Monsters avoid or be repulsed by the aura they emit, encouraging people to create Heroes that would go and kill the direct descendants of the Dark Goddess, known only as the Monster Lord.

### **Dark Goddess Viyella - Embodiment of Dark**

Often associated with evil by humans, Viyella is the ancestor of all monsters, her presence is not as prominent as Aryllis in the world, and she is considered almost a legend amongst monsters given that she hasn't been seen or heard of for eons.

As the embodiment of Dark, she stands in direct opposition to the goddess of Light Aryllis, The two of them stand as the main Goddesses of the world.

Viyella, contrary to Aryllis, isn't really known to hate non-monsters, however, as is the way of monsters, she does believe in Might makes right, with her standing at the top, believing that the monsters can rule themselves, and isn't really involved in guidance, or anything really, only intervening when Aryllis threatens her subjects, or so it was said that she behaved, before vanishing.

While she does not present herself as a god in the traditional sense, her power is absolutely comparable to that of Aryllis, simply different in nature. Opposite to her, Viyella had plenty of descendants, stemming from her own Blood, and branching off into 6 Mighty Monsters known as the 6 Ancestors, who Became the initial branches from which all monsters came from, with the one that Had the closest ties to her being Elyph II , becoming the Second Monster Lord, after the disappearance of Viyella, continuing the bloodline for generations, even as the mighty power wasn't the same.


### **Sylph - Embodiment of Wind**

A playful Fairy-looking being that seems to be perfectly attuned to the wind, who is said to live in the Hallowed Forest near San Aryllis, she is only really known by monsters and humans who live nearby, although for humans, she is more of a rumour that goes around.

Sylph is one of the 4 Natural elements that took form over millennia, different from the two primordial Goddesses, she came into existence only after the holy monster wars, as the elemental wind energy became concentrated in one point, and one day, gained a consciousness.

Sylph doesn't really see herself as a goddess of any kind, mostly a force of nature. The monsters who inhabit the forest she lives in keep guard to protect her, despite them being weaker, due to how important she is to the ecosystem, as her death would cause the winds to go wild, or cease completely. There are some tales about her making contracts with humans who come meet her to grant them her power, only if they prove worthy. Heinrich Hain, the legendary hero, is said to have formed a contract with all 4 Natural elements in his travels.

Her power, while minor compared to the Dark and Light embodiments, is directly connected to the world, as she is said to be able to sense when there are disturbances that affect the wind...

### **Gnome - Embodiment of Earth**

A mute being that is said to wear clothes that block his mouth, dwarven in appearance, who seems perfectly attuned to Stone and earth. It is said that he inhabits ruins somewhere deep in the Sabasa Desert, sometimes seen playing with clay dolls. He is only really known by monsters and humans who live nearby, although for humans, He is more of a rumour that goes around.

Gnome is one of the 4 Natural elements that took form over millennia, different from the two primordial Goddesses, he came into existence only after the holy monster wars, as the elemental earth energy became concentrated in one point, and one day, gained a consciousness.

Gnome does not care for matters of religion, as he spends most of his time alone with his clay dolls. Monsters that roam the desert tend to avoid the ruins he lives in, as they find him creepy. There are some tales about him making contracts with humans who come meet him to grant them his power, only if they prove worthy. Heinrich Hain, the legendary hero, is said to have formed a contract with all 4 Natural elements in his travels.

His power, while minor compared to the Dark and Light embodiments, is directly connected to the world, as he is said to be able to sense when there are disturbances that affect the earth, and his demise would mean large earthquakes of undetermined duration that would wreak the world. 

### **Undyne - Embodiment of Water**

A cold being that appears to have the body akin to that of a slime, while holding the shape of a mermaid, who seems perfectly attuned to water. Her location is known to very few humans, and monsters are not quick to disclose her home. However, some tongues say that you can safely enter a pond that irradiates magic near Grand Noah.

Undyne is one of the 4 Natural elements that took form over millennia, different from the two primordial Goddesses, she came into existence only after the holy monster wars, as the elemental water energy became concentrated in one point, and one day, gained a consciousness.

Undyne is not really seen as a god of any sort, but more so a guardian of the water creatures, particularly slimes, as she harbours a great hatred towards humanity for constantly being careless and polluting the waters and aims to keeps her home as a safe haven where the water is pristine and pure at all times. There are some tales about her making contracts with humans who come meet her to grant them her power, only if they prove worthy. Heinrich Hain, the legendary hero, is said to have formed a contract with all 4 Natural elements in his travels.

Her power, while minor compared to the Dark and Light embodiments, is directly connected to the world, as she is said to be able to control the tides and flow of water, her demise would mean for the oceans to become completely still and become unable to harbour any sort of life.

### **Salamander - Embodiment of Fire**

A burning lizard that has rune-like tattoos all over their body is said to live deep inside the volcano present in the Gold Region, who seems perfectly attuned to fire. His existence is somewhat of an old legend as very few dare tread into the hellish insides of the volcano's caverns, that is, except some monsters who enjoy such high temperatures.

Salamander is one of the 4 Natural elements that took form over millennia, different from the two primordial Goddesses, he came into existence only after the holy monster wars, as the elemental fire energy became concentrated in one point, and one day, gained a consciousness.

Salamander doesn't really consider himself much of a god, rather, he is much more keen on the idea of being a master, due to his fiery and intense personality, combat is one of his best aptitudes, some rumours even say, that one of the 4 Heavenly Knights trained directly under him. There are some tales about him making contracts with humans who come meet him to grant them his power, only if they prove worthy. Heinrich Hain, the legendary hero, is said to have formed a contract with all 4 Natural elements in his travels.

His power, while minor compared to the Dark and Light embodiments, is directly connected to the world, although different from his Natural counterparts, his specialization lies in bringing out Explosive potential in people, at a cost. If he were to die, volcanos would erupt, and there'd be a massive decrease in global temperatures.
