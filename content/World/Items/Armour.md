
## Body Armour
### Clothes

Clothes can be used by all races, but their defences are lacking in exchange for their utility.

#### Regular clothes

Everyday Clothes used by most non-adventurers. Not really suited for combat

DEF +2
WIL +1

#### Makeshift Black Cape

Flimsy cape worn by low ranking Vampires. Passable at night, but just sad when worn in broad daylight.

DEF +1
WIL +2

### Gi

Gi are the iconic gear of a martial artist and, in addition to increasing Defence and Willpower, increase Agility and Dexterity.
#### Training Vest

Clothing for beginner martial artists

DEF +3
WIL +2
DEX +3
AGI +2

### Breastplates

A sturdy piece of armour meant to protect the chest area. Increases Defence and Willpower are like most armours, but nothing special aside from generally being better than clothes and regular armour. They are more expensive than both clothes and regular armour, however.

No Starter Breastplates

### Armour 

Standard piece of Armour. Perfectly average.

#### Leather Armour

Basic Armour made from animal hide.

DEF +5
WIL +3



### Heavy Armour

Usually the strongest pieces of Armour you'll come across in terms of Defence and willpower, Heavy Armour cannot be used by most jobs and races

No starter Heavy Armours

### Shells

Intended for insects, shells boost their absurd Defences even higher.

#### Turtle Shell

Large domed armour that protects one's back.

DEF +12
WIL +3



### Robes

Whether a healer or a damage dealer, you'll want to give your casters a robe to boost their power. Many robes are light on physical Defence but come with high Will and Magic with the best ones also having element boosters or resistances.

#### Cotton Robe

Cotton robe for novice mages.

DEF +2
MAG +4
WIL +4



### Dresses

Dresses are the favoured gear of dancers and singers due to their high boost to dexterity compared to other armour.

#### Leather Dress

Simple dress made from animal hide

DEF +5
WIL +5
DEX +5



### Skimpy

Demons of the night wind prefer their clothing to be more revealing to better entrance their prey. Their abilities rely on dexterity and these clothes give a nice boost.

No Starter Skimpy

## Head Armour

### Hats

They're hats. What do you expect them to do? They're cheap and if you have nothing better to wear, might as well wear a hat.

#### Strong Hat

A sturdy hat, but it doesn't give much defence in combat

DEF +2
WIL +1
AGI +1



### Helmets

The average piece of headgear. Some of them come with a boost to attack.

#### Bronze Helm

Cheap and not very strong helmet made of bronze

DEF +6
WIL +3


### Heavy Helmets

Similar to heavy armour, Heavy Helmets are the best head gear in terms of Defence and willpower. They're also the most expensive.

No starter Heavy Helmets

### Magic Hats

Your wizards have their robes, now they need their wizard hats. Same fair, light increase to Defence, bigger to willpower and magic. May come with boosters to elements.

#### Priest Hat

Basic hat liked by novice mages.

DEF +3
MAG +6
WIL +6


### Fancy Hats

Who needs Defence when you have style? Actually not horrible Defences but it's the increased dexterity you'll equip these for.

No Starter Fancy Hats


## Off-hand Shields

### Bucklers

The smallest shield. Equippable by most but don't expect a significant difference.

All bucklers provide:

- Evasion +5%
- Magic Evasion +5%

#### Wooden Shield

Very light wooden shield. Light, but doesn't provide much protection.

DEF +3
WIL +2


### Shields

The standard choice of most warriors.

All Shields Provide:

- Evasion +10%
- Magic Evasion +10%

#### Hide Shield

Basic shield made from animal hide

DEF +2
WIL +1



### Heavy Shields

The best protection you can get in one hand. 

All Heavy Shields Provide:

- Evasion +15%
- Magic Evasion +15%

No Starter Heavy Shields.
