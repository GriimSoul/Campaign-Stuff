#### Cities
##### 1. Aryllisville

![[AryllisVille.png]]

The town where the party will meet.

It is a small and peaceful village where travellers sometimes stop by, most shops here don't have anything that could serve adventurers, at most the local blacksmith could make a sword, even if most of his life has been dedicated to farming equipment.

It is home to the second largest Aryllis Temple , with the first one being San Aryllis itself, where people used to come and receive baptism from Aryllis herself, back in the day at least.
Within the temple they possess a priest capable of changing jobs.
