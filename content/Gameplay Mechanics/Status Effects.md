
During battle you may be affected by a wide variety of status effects, Consider this a compendium of what each of them means gameplay wise, both in effects and common means of getting rid of them.

## ![[Death.png]] Insta-Death

### Death

Makes the affected's body instantaneously stop responding, making all cells within them perish, effectively killing them. The soul can be brought back if combat  hasn't ended
### Ascension

Makes the one affected become engulfed in holy light, having their physical body disappear, and leaving only their soul to linger until combat ends, if not revived in time, the soul will ascend as well.

## Common Status Effects

### ![[Poison.png]] Poison

**Effect**: Lose 5% of your Max HP every round at the end of your turn
**Duration**: 5 Rounds
**Cure Methods**: Antidote, Grass, Panacea

### ![[Blind.png]] Blind

**Effect**: Disadvantage on Attack, Evasion, Critical, Counter, Reflect, Rolls. Disables All skills with the "Demon Eyes" Prefix.
**Duration**: 5 Rounds
**Cure Methods:** Eye drops, Panacea

### ![[Silence.png]] Silence

**Effect:** Seals magic skills and prevents their use (Includes Dancing, Singing, Converse, Demon Arts and Nature Skills)
**Duration:** 6 Rounds
**Cure Methods:** Echo Grass, Panacea

### ![[Confusion.png]] Confusion

**Effect:** 50/50 chance of hitting enemies or allies, has a 10% chance of being removed upon being hit.
**Duration:** 3 Turns
**Cure Methods**: Restorative, Panacea

### ![[Sleep.png]] Sleep

**Effect:** Prevents all actions. Negates Evasion, Magic Evasion and Counters. Has a 25% chance of being removed when taking damage
**Duration:** 3 Rounds
**Cure Methods:** Restorative,, Panacea

### ![[Paralysis.png]] Paralysis

**Effect:** Prevents all actions. Negates Evasion, Magic Evasion and Counters.
**Duration:** 2 Rounds
**Cure Methods:** Full Moon Grass, Panacea

### ![[Stun.png]] Stun

**Effect:** Prevents all actions. Negates Evasion, Magic Evasion, and Counters.
**Duration:** 1 Round
**Cure Methods:** None

### ![[Bind.png]] Bind

**Effect:** Prevents all actions. Negates Evasion, Magic Evasion, and Counters.
**Duration:** Indefinite | Until Success
**Cure Methods:** Success on an ATK check against whoever Bound you, every attempt after the first one gains a +4 cumulatively.

## Rare Status Effects

### ![[Burn.png]] Burn

**Effect:** Lose 10% of your Max HP at the end of your turn.
**Duration:** 1 Round
**Cure Methods:** Panacea, Literal WATER

### ![[Freeze.png]] Freeze

**Effect:** Prevents all actions. Negates Evasion, Magic Evasion, and Counters.
**Duration:** 2 Rounds
**Cure Methods:** Panacea, the warmth of fire

### ![[Shock.png]] Shock

**Effect:** Take 10% more Lightning Damage
**Duration:** 2 Rounds
**Cure Methods:** Panacea, Hitting the Earth.

### ![[Slow.png]] Slow

**Effect:** Reduces Agility, Evasion, and Magic Evasion by 50%, also reduces Hit Rate by 20%
**Duration:** 5 Turns
**Cure Methods:** Quick Spell, Stop Spell

### ![[Stop.png]] Stop

**Effect:** Prevents all actions. Negates Evasion, Magic Evasion and Counters.
**Duration:** 3 Turns
**Cure Methods:** Quick Spell, Slow Spell

### ![[Mini.png]] Mini

**Effect:** Reduces ATK, DEF and AGI by 50%
**Duration:** 3 Rounds
**Cure Methods:** Lucky Mallet, Panacea

### ![[Zombie.png]] Zombie

**Effect:** Take damage equal to 300% of what a healing item/effect would normally restore, but become immune to Poison, get healed by Bio attacks, and fully healed by Instant Death, and revival items/effects function as Instant Death.
**Duration:** Indefinite
**Cure Methods:** Holy Water. Panacea (Can only be cured if this effect doesn't come from their race)

### ![[Petrify.png]] Petrify

**Effect:** Pseudo-Death, entirely out of combat until cured
**Duration:** Indefinite, remains after combat
**Cure Methods:** Gold Needle, Panacea

### ![[Digest.png]] Digest

**Effect:** Loses 5% of Max HP each turn. Causes predation attacks to kill instantly
**Duration:** Indefinite
**Cure Methods:** Panacea, Wiping yourself with a cloth (The cloth is destroyed in the process)

### ![[Slimed.png]] Slimed

**Effect:** Increases Damage taken from Psychological, Fire, Lightning and Ice element Attacks by 50%
**Duration:**  5 Rounds
**Cure Methods:** Panacea, Wiping yourself with a cloth (The cloth is destroyed in the process)

## Psychological Status effects

### ![[Horny.png]] Horny

**Effect:**  Increases Damage taken from Psychological type attacks by 100%
**Duration:** Indefinite
**Cure Methods:** De-Love Potion, Panacea

### ![[Trance.png]] Trance

**Effect:** Prevents all actions. Negates Evasion, Magic Evasion, and Counters.
**Duration:** 2 Rounds 
**Cure Methods:** De-Love Potion, Panacea

### ![[Seduction.png]] Seduction

**Effect:** Attacks Allies as if they were its enemies, becomes absolutely enthralled with the Seducer.
**Duration:** 2 Rounds
**Cure Methods:** De-Love Potion, Panacea

### ![[Incontience.png]] Incontinence

**Effect:**  Negates Evasion, Magic Evasion, and increases damage taken from Psychological type attacks by 50%
**Duration:** 3 Rounds
**Cure Methods:** De-Love Potion, Panacea.

## Other Status Effects

### ![[Quick.png]] Quick

**Effect:**  Increases Agility, Evasion, and Magic Evasion by 50%, also Increases Hit Rate by 20%
**Duration:** 6 Turns
**Cure Methods:** Slow Spell, Stop Spell, Dispel Spell

### ![[Berserk.png]] Berserk

**Effect:** Increases Attack by 50% and SP Regeneration by 10%. Attacks are chosen at random from the pool of Skill Adaptation and regular attacks.
**Duration:** Indefinite
**Cure Methods:** Dispel Spell, Being knocked out, Rampage

### ![[Berserk.png]] Rampage

**Effect:** Increase Attack by 100% and SP Regeneration by 25%. Gains an additional Action. Attacks are chosen at random from the pool of Skill Adaptation and regular attacks. Nullifies Berserk
**Duration:** Indefinite
**Cure Methods:** Dispel Spell, Being Knocked out
